"Children are the world's most valuable resource and the best hope for the future." – J.F. Kennedy

"The children of today will make the India of tomorrow." – Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru

The children of today will shape the future of the country. Their upbringing determines the country's future. Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru emphasized that the way we nurture them shapes the future. Education is crucial, and in today's world, it's a key for growth and success.

Tapasvi, with the ideology of providing modern education to economically deprived and challenged children, aims to create well-rounded individuals through education, life skills, and value development.

Our daily school routine starts with prayer, meditation, and motivational stories, ensuring the overall development of students. Our focus is on education, values, and mental growth, which prepares them for a successful future.

Basic Necessities

Tapasvi’s "Akshara Jyothi Project" supports education for children in slums. The project aims to make a difference by providing educational materials and tutoring to children living in difficult conditions.

As part of this initiative, we have provided books, stationery, and bags to children in need. We also run awareness campaigns for their parents to emphasize the importance of education for their children's future.

Value Education for Children

The objective of our value education project is to equip children with the right attitude and values to face the outer world. We conduct personality development sessions, games, arts, crafts, and spiritual teachings to instill positive growth in children.

Trainings and Workshops

We offer various workshops to develop life skills and leadership qualities. These workshops focus on practical skills, like communication, self-defense, and emotional intelligence.
